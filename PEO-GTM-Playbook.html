PEO Go-To-Market Playbook

Launching PEO into the payroll base — a partner-led, AI-targeted monetization motion
Prepared by Gabe Revnew · Companion to the live PEO Targeting Agent demo

The opportunity — expansion, not new logos

Gusto has 500,000+ SMB payroll customers. A meaningful share already sit in the PEO sweet spot — 10–250 employees, multi-state, benefits gaps, compliance-heavy industries. The PEO motion is a monetization layer on the installed base, not a greenfield land grab. A warm base means dramatically lower CAC and faster payback than new-logo selling. Sizing: fit-eligible accounts × worksite employees × gross profit per WSE = the prize; the targeting engine estimates the fit-eligible set, finance sets the attach-rate target.

The motion — three pillars

1 Lead pillar

Accountant & advisor channel

Gusto already owns the trusted-advisor relationship through its tiered (Silver/Gold/Platinum) partner program and People Advisory. Accountants and brokers are the warm front door to PEO. Activate the highest-fit partner books first, certify and incentivize advisors to co-sell, and build partner-sourced and partner-influenced pipeline.

2

Specialist overlay + base routing

PEO is a consultative, co-employment sale — not a payroll add-on. A PEO specialist team is fed by PEO-Qualified Leads from three sources: the AI propensity score on the base, AM/CSM signals, and accountant referrals — with a defined PQL and routing SLA.

3

AI targeting & enablement engine

The PEO Targeting Agent scores and ranks the base, qualifies/disqualifies with reasons, briefs and coaches reps, drafts motion-aware outreach, ranks advisor books by fit, and learns from every outcome. The force multiplier under all three pillars.

Lead flow

Base propensity score+ Accountant referral+ AM / CSM signal→ PEO-Qualified Lead→ PEO specialist→ Co-sell with advisor

Phased rollout

Crawl — pilot

One high-fit cohort (comp-heavy + multi-state + on Gusto + hiring) and a handful of Prioritize-tier accountants. Prove win rate, CAC payback, and time-to-value.

Walk — expand

Add cohorts and states; scale People Advisory PEO certification; tune the score against real won/lost outcomes.

Run — scale

Full base and channel; automate targeting (nightly scan surfaces top accounts to reps and partners).

Metrics scorecard

PEO attach rate on base PEO net revenue retention CAC payback (upsell) Win rate by fit band Time-to-value Partner-sourced / influenced %

 

Guardrail — protect the core

  • Do no harm to the payroll relationship — a botched co-employment transition can churn the base.
  • Disqualify bad-fit and bad-risk accounts to protect the master workers’ comp policy.
  • Obsess over implementation and time-to-value.
  • Responsible AI: it gathers facts, transparent rules decide, humans verify — no fabricated numbers or customer stories.
